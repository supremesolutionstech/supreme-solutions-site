About Supreme Solutions

Supreme Solutions was founded by two cybersecurity professionals with one mission — to make high-quality IT management and security accessible to every business, not just large corporations. We believe every small business deserves enterprise-level protection, automation, and peace of mind.

style="text-align: center; Our Story

The idea for Supreme Solutions began when co-founder Malik Browne, an Information Technology graduate and CompTIA Security+ certified professional, worked on healthcare technology implementations in partnership with Cone Health. Malik saw firsthand how many organizations struggled to maintain secure, efficient systems — often without dedicated IT staff.

Around the same time, co-founder Anthony Scott was serving in the U.S. Navy, managing cybersecurity operations aboard submarines. His hands-on experience protecting mission-critical systems inspired him to bring military-grade discipline and defense strategies to the business world.

Together, they combined their technical expertise and passion for innovation to create Supreme Solutions — a next-generation managed service provider (MSP) powered by AI automation and human expertise.

Malik Browne — Co-Founder & IT Security Lead

Malik specializes in network security, system administration, and automation. With a strong foundation in IT and a Security+ certification, he focuses on delivering reliable, scalable solutions to clients across healthcare, retail, and service industries.

Anthony Scott — Co-Founder & Cyber Operations Lead

A Navy cybersecurity specialist, Anthony brings real-world defense experience and mission-critical discipline to SafeDesk IT. His background in securing classified systems ensures every client benefits from the same level of security trusted by the military.

Our Mission

To simplify technology for businesses by blending AI-driven automation with human expertise. We help companies stay protected, productive, and prepared for whatever comes next.
